# /fill-form

Fill out a form using your FarmAdvisor data. Upload a form (PDF, image, or document) and this command will:

1. Select your organization (auto-selects your default if you have multiple)
2. Load your properties
3. Map your farm data to the form fields

## Usage

```
/fill-form
```

Then upload or paste your form.
